What We Do

At DONCASSA TECHNOLOGIES LTD., our expertise lies in delivering advanced Compressed Natural Gas (CNG) technologies. We provide complete solutions for natural gas system installation and maintenance, including: Installing natural gas compressors and pressure reducing and metering stations Setting up CNG-powered generators for efficient energy solutions Converting diesel and petrol vehicles to natural gas vehicles (NGVs) Our goal is to help our clients achieve energy independence and reduce their power generation costs, all while supporting a cleaner and greener future.
